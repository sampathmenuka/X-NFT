Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

To create an NFT, connect your wallet, navigate to the "Create" section, upload your digital asset, add details like title and description, set your price, and mint your NFT. Our platform supports various file formats including images, videos, and audio.

We support MetaMask, WalletConnect, Coinbase Wallet, and Trust Wallet. Simply click "Connect Wallet" and choose your preferred wallet provider to get started.

X-NFT charges a 2.5% service fee on successful sales. Additionally, blockchain gas fees apply for minting and transactions. Creators can set their own royalty percentages for secondary sales.

Our support team typically responds within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please reach out via our live chat feature for faster assistance.
